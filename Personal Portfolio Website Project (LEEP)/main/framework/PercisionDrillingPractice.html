Oil and Gas Field Trip to Precision Drilling

Learning Drilling Operations, Hand Signals, Well-Killing Procedure, and BOP Devices

About This Project

This field trip to Precision Drilling provided hands-on experience in the operation of drilling machines. We also learned important safety procedures such as operator hand signals for communication, techniques for killing a well, and how Blowout Preventer (BOP) devices are used in the field to prevent blowouts.

Key Learnings

  • Operating a drilling machine safely and efficiently
  • Understanding and using operator hand signals for communication on-site
  • Learning the procedure for killing a well to stop a well from uncontrollably releasing oil or gas
  • Using Blowout Preventer (BOP) devices to prevent blowouts and maintain safety
