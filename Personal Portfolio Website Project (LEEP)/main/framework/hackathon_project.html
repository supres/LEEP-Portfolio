Hackathon Project - Vet4Net

Connecting Veterans and Military Personnel to Essential Resources Using AI

About This Project

This project was part of a 3-week hackathon hosted by the Blacks at Microsoft apprenticeship program. The challenge was to develop a technology-driven solution for the social good of society, with a strong emphasis on AI. Our team created **Vet4Net**, an app designed to connect current military personnel and veterans to various social services like doctors, psychiatrists, therapists, and other resources that would help them thrive.

Key Features of Vet4Net

  • AI-powered matchmaking system that connects veterans with healthcare providers, mental health professionals, and support services.
  • Customized recommendations based on individual needs, location, and urgency of the situation.
  • User-friendly interface with a community-driven feature for social interaction and peer support.
  • Focus on improving the quality of life for veterans and military personnel by removing barriers to essential services.

Technologies Used

  • AI and Machine Learning for the recommendation engine
  • Mobile App Development (iOS/Android)
  • Cloud Infrastructure for data storage and access
  • User Experience (UX) Design and Research
